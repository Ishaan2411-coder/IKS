The Beginning

The Story Behind IKS

It started with a dream — an animated movie, a whole universe of stories waiting to be told. But there was one problem: ₹0 in the budget.

The animated movie became a book. The big team became a solo journey. But the dream? The dream never died.

That's when it hit — if one creator struggles alone, imagine what happens when creators come together. Not for money, not for clout, but for the pure joy of building something together.

And so, IKS was born — a community where your skills are your currency, your passion is your investment, and your dreams are never too big to chase.

"Great dreams should not fail because of a lack of money, resources, or connections."

Our Code

Community Rules

1 No Money Involved
IKS is built on collaboration, not transactions. Members must not ask for, demand, or offer money for contributions within community projects.
2 Studies Come First
Most members are students. Your education, family responsibilities, and personal growth always come first. Participate only in your free time and, where required, with your parents' permission.
3 Voluntary Participation
No member is forced to work on any project. If you are not interested, unavailable, or uncomfortable with a task, you have the right to respectfully say "No."
4 Family-Like Environment
We want IKS to feel like a supportive family. Healthy jokes, fun conversations, and friendly interactions are welcome. However, personal attacks, bullying, harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or disrespectful comments are strictly prohibited.
5 Respect Every Dream
Behind every project is someone's dream. Whether you are a writer, artist, editor, animator, musician, developer, or creator — contribute honestly and responsibly. We are not just building projects — we are helping people build their dreams.
6 Give Credit Where It's Due
Always acknowledge and respect the work of fellow creators. Do not copy, steal, repost, or claim someone else's work as your own.
7 Keep the Community Safe
Any form of scams, threats, harmful behavior, inappropriate content, or activities that may harm members or the community will not be tolerated.
8 Learn, Help, Grow
The purpose of IKS is not competition. It is collaboration. Help others when you can, learn when you need to, and grow together as creators.
9 No Ghosting After Commitment
If you commit to a project and later become unavailable, inform the team as soon as possible. Do not disappear without notice. Respect everyone's time and effort.
10 Use of IKS Name & Responsibility
No individual, team, or project may use the IKS name, logo, branding, or claim official association with IKS without the knowledge and approval of the Founder. Any project created without such approval is considered independent and unofficial.
11 Rules May Evolve
As IKS grows, new situations and challenges may arise. These rules may be updated, expanded, or modified whenever necessary to ensure the safety, growth, and integrity of the community.
